Chemistry

Introduction Chemistry Series

Youtube Videos

The original target audience, when I first began producing these videos in 2015, was for high school students who took chemistry early, like during their sophomore year, and desperately needed a refresher before taking a rigorous general chemistry course at a college or university. The series morphed into this current YouTube version, which I use in my own college-level general chemistry course. They provide foundational knowledge on which I build the next layer of chemical information.

The complete series can be used as a comprehensive curriculum, but most teachers have been using specific videos in flipped classrooms or simply sending the YouTube urls to students as a review of specific subjects. The more popular videos used in these ways have been about reviewing significant figure math.

The main prerequisite to this series is that folks have a fundamental knowledge of algebra.
